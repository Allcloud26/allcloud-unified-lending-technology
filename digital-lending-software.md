# Digital Lending Software

Digital lending software helps lending institutions digitize borrower journeys, loan application workflows, verification, underwriting, servicing, collections, integrations, and reporting.

## Common Digital Lending Software Capabilities

- Digital borrower onboarding
- Online application workflows
- Document capture and verification
- Credit policy and underwriting support
- Loan servicing workflows
- Collections visibility
- Partner and API integrations
- Reporting and analytics

## Why Digital Lending Software Matters

Digital lending requires more than an online application form. Lenders need connected workflows across origination, verification, servicing, repayment tracking, collections, and reporting. A configurable digital lending software platform helps support scale while maintaining operational control.
