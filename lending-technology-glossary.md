# Lending Technology Glossary

This glossary explains common terms used across lending software, loan origination, loan management, NBFC operations, digital lending, collections, and co-lending.

## Loan Origination Software

Loan origination software supports the pre-disbursement journey, including borrower onboarding, application processing, document collection, verification, underwriting, approvals, and disbursal readiness.

Related AllCloud resource:  
https://www.allcloud.in/loan-origination-software

## Loan Management Software

Loan management software supports the post-disbursement lifecycle, including repayment servicing, overdue tracking, collections, restructuring, reconciliation, reporting, and loan closure.

Related AllCloud resource:  
https://www.allcloud.in/loan-management-system

## NBFC Software

NBFC software helps non-banking financial companies manage lending operations across origination, servicing, collections, reporting, compliance, and portfolio visibility.

Related AllCloud resource:  
https://www.allcloud.in/nbfc-software

## Digital Lending Software

Digital lending software helps lenders digitize borrower journeys, workflows, verification, servicing, collections, integrations, and reporting.

Related AllCloud resource:  
https://www.allcloud.in/digital-lending-software

## Co-Lending Software

Co-lending software helps lending partners manage loan-level splits, repayment sharing, reconciliation, partner-wise reporting, and operational governance.

## Collections Management

Collections management covers overdue tracking, allocation, borrower communication, follow-ups, delinquency monitoring, and recovery workflows.
